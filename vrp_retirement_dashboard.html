Knowledge Base — Quick Answers

Lump Sum & Pay
When do I get my lump sum?▶
Before end of July 2026. Via direct deposit to your regular pay accounts within 30 days of termination. The plan document specifies no later than March 15, 2027 as the absolute deadline, but in practice it will be by end of July.
Verify your direct deposit accounts are current before July 1.
Will my lump sum have 401(k) or HSA deductions?▶
No. The VRP lump sum is not considered regular pay. Voluntary deductions like 401(k) and HSA are NOT taken. Applicable taxes ARE withheld (at supplemental rate). Any debts owed to Microsoft may be deducted.
Am I eligible for the FY26 annual bonus?▶
Yes. VRP participants are employed through end of FY26 and receive the annual bonus based on impact level. FY26 bonuses are paid in September 2026.
Update your 401(k) elections by July 1 to have contributions taken from the bonus. Microsoft will match up to $12,250.
How do I access pay stubs after corpnet ends?▶
Register at my.adp.com using code MSFT-PAY or the "Find Me" option (email, name, DOB, last 4 SSN). Do this before your last day to ensure access.
Healthcare & COBRA
When does healthcare coverage end?▶
July 1, 2026 — your last day. If you enroll in COBRA within the 60-day window, coverage continues retroactively from July 2 with no gap in coverage.
What happens if I miss COBRA enrollment?▶
You permanently lose eligibility for COBRA, all 5 years of VRP Extended Healthcare, AND the 12-month Microsoft subsidy. Non-recoverable.
⚠️ Packet arrives week of July 6. Enroll at cobra.me.microsoft.com (key: "microsoft") starting July 3. Your exact deadline is in the packet.
How much does COBRA cost and who pays?▶
Microsoft pays 100% for Months 1–12 (through June 30, 2027). Starting Month 13 (July 2027), you pay full COBRA rates. Health Savings Plan (Premera) Employee Only = $836.40/mo; with spouse = $1,672.80/mo. Rates update each November. You can use HSA funds to pay COBRA premiums.
What is VRP Extended Healthcare vs COBRA?▶
COBRA covers months 1–18 (July 2, 2026 → Jan 1, 2028). VRP Extended Healthcare automatically takes over for months 19–60 (Jan 2, 2028 → July 1, 2031) — same benefits, same COBRA rates. No separate enrollment; it's automatic if you're on COBRA. Total: up to 5 years continuous.
Can I use HSA funds to pay COBRA premiums?▶
Yes. COBRA and VRP Extended Healthcare premiums are qualified medical expenses eligible for HSA payment or reimbursement. Consult your tax advisor for details on how to properly record this.
Stock Vesting
What happens to my unvested stock?▶
Two tracks: (1) FY21–FY24 Annual Awards — you qualify for Stock Plan Retirement, so these continue vesting on their full original schedule indefinitely. (2) FY25 Annual Award — only eligible for the 12-month VRP window (through July 2, 2027) because it was granted less than 12 months before separation. 63 FY25 shares vest; 162 are forfeited after July 2, 2027. Total unvested vesting: 936 shares (~$375K at $401).
When is the first post-retirement vest?▶
August 31, 2026 — 145 shares from FY21+FY22+FY23+FY24+FY25 awards, estimated at ~$58K at $401/share. Make sure your Fidelity brokerage is set up to receive shares before then.
Will I receive the FY26 Annual Stock Award?▶
No. The FY26 Annual Stock Award grant date is August 31, 2026. You must be actively employed on the grant date. Your employment ends July 2, 2026 — before the grant date.
How do I access stock info after I leave?▶
Email Stock@Microsoft.com — they handle stock-related queries after employment ends. Also ensure your brokerage (Fidelity) account is set up and accessible without corpnet before July 1.
Download your TRP stock Excel report BEFORE July 1 — TRP access ends with employment.
Rules & Restrictions
Can I come back to work at Microsoft?▶
Not for 2 years as a full-time employee. Not for 6 months as a contractor. You don't need to repay the lump sum if you return after 2 years. However, returning as an employee and enrolling in Microsoft healthcare would end your COBRA/VRP Extended Healthcare coverage.
Am I eligible for unemployment benefits?▶
Likely not. As part of VRP, you resign voluntarily. Voluntary retirements are generally not eligible for unemployment benefits, though it ultimately depends on your state's laws. Washington State may have specific rules — consult an employment attorney or the WA Employment Security Department if this matters to you.
Can I still rescind my VRP election?▶
Yes — if you signed the VRSAR, you can rescind it during the June 23–29, 2026 window at aka.ms/VRP. After June 29, the separation is final. Rescinding means no VRP benefits and you remain an at-will employee.
What post-employment obligations remain?▶
Your Non-Disclosure Agreement and Employee Agreement remain fully binding. You must return all Microsoft property by July 1 and permanently delete all Microsoft property from personal devices. If you have a US security clearance, contact myclearance@microsoft.com to be offboarded properly.
What if I resign before July 2?▶
You lose all VRP benefits. The separation date is fixed at July 2, 2026 and cannot be moved. You are expected to work through July 1. You can take discretionary time off with manager approval, but you must remain employed through separation.
Medicare — Kamaljit & Davinder
When does Kamaljit's VRP Extended Healthcare end?▶
March 13, 2028 — your 65th birthday. Per the plan, VRP Extended Healthcare ends when you become eligible for Medicare (typically age 65), regardless of whether you actually enroll.
Enroll in Medicare before March 13, 2028 so coverage starts March 1 with no gap. Your Initial Enrollment Period runs Dec 13, 2027 – Jun 13, 2028.
What happens to Davinder's coverage when Kamaljit enrolls in Medicare?▶
Kamaljit's VRP Extended Healthcare ends, but Davinder can continue COBRA independently. Under a special COBRA rule, Kamaljit's Medicare enrollment within the first 18 months of COBRA is a secondary qualifying event for Davinder — potentially extending her independent COBRA up to 36 months from Kamaljit's Medicare eligibility date (March 13, 2028 → March 13, 2031). Contact BSC immediately after enrollment to protect this right. Davinder pays full COBRA premiums independently.
What is the Medigap guaranteed-issue window and why does it matter?▶
The Medigap Open Enrollment Period is a 6-month window starting when you enroll in Medicare Part B. During this window, insurers cannot deny coverage or charge more due to pre-existing conditions. After this window, insurers can reject you. For Kamaljit: opens ~March 1, 2028. For Davinder: opens ~October 1, 2029. Do not miss these windows — guaranteed issue only happens once.
Is Davinder eligible for premium-free Medicare Part A?▶
Very likely yes, via spousal benefit. Part A is premium-free if you or your spouse paid Medicare taxes for at least 10 years (40 quarters). With Kamaljit's 30+ years at Microsoft, Davinder almost certainly qualifies. Confirm with the Social Security Administration (SSA.gov or 1-800-772-1213).

• Social Security Administration — SSA.gov or 1-800-772-1213 for Part A/B enrollment.
Start planning 6 months before each 65th birthday: Sep 2027 for Kamaljit, Apr 2029 for Davinder.
